Profile

Han Li

Senior Staff Machine Learning Researcher, Shopify

I am a machine learning researcher at Shopify. My work focuses on agentic foundation models, vision-language modeling, and user simulation for AI agents. Before joining Shopify, I was an Applied Scientist at Amazon Stores Foundational AI, where I led efforts in large language model pretraining, as well as vision-language model pretraining and post-training.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Sciences from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, advised by Prof. AnHai Doan, and received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University.
